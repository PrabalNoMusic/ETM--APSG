Analysis

Beyond its scenic allure, sunder nursery has evolved into a catalyst for economic growth while preserving cultural heritage and promoting environmental sustainability Sunder nursery was restored by the aga khan trust for culture. One of the key components of Nizamuddin Urban Renewal initiative is environmental development and restoring the historic urban landscapes. To fulfil this purpose, the landscape master plan for environmental development of sunder nursery was prepared by shaheer associates in close consultation with the CPWD- AKTC project team, which received formal ASL, MCD, DUAC and CPWD approval.



Revenue Streams

The revenue streams used in the Sunder Nursery are:

  • Car Parking: 100/- for 4 hours, Weekdays, 200/- for 4 hours on Sundays and Gazetted holidays.
  • Two-Wheeler Parking: 35/- for 4 hours
  • The government sunder nursery management trust(GSNMT) offers sunder nursery - parks as a venue for various cultural, educational, and ecological public programmes.
  • Memberships
