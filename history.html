Learning Through Monuments.



About some of monuments in sunder nursery-

Three of the nine Mughal-era buildings in Sunder Nursery are: the Sundarwala Burj, Sundarwala Mahal, and the Lakkarwala Burj, they are regarded as being of national importance. As per the UNESCO revision from 2016, these three sites are also included in Humayun's Tomb World Heritage Site, which makes it one of India's densest ensembles of 16th-century tomb gardens.

Using traditional methods, techniques, and materials, skilled craftsmen collaborate with architects and engineers to replicate the work of their predecessors in the conservation of historic monuments. This approach strengthens the age-old building abilities. Large-scale conservation efforts have included fixing Sundarwala Mahal's severe structural issues as well as restoring the original, elaborate plaster and ornamental patterns at the Sundarwala and Lakkarwala Burj.
